# Kill Rules

## Creative Kill Rules

Pause a creative if:

- Link CTR stays below `0.8%`.
- CPC is above USD `1.00` equivalent with no AddToCart.
- Comments show strong trust or shipping objections and no buying intent.
- 100 sessions produce AddToCart below `3%`.

## Ad Set Kill Rules

Pause an ad set if:

- NGN `5,000` spend produces no AddToCart.
- CPM is unusually high and CTR is weak.
- Landing-page views are low compared to link clicks.

## Campaign Kill Rules

Pause campaign if:

- NGN `15,000` total spend produces no AddToCart.
- Checkout starts occur but no payment and comments mention payment distrust.
- Account receives policy warning.

## Do Not Kill Too Early

Do not kill based on:

- Low views alone.
- No purchases before there are enough sessions.
- One bad comment.
