# Scaling Rules

Scale only after there is buying intent.

## Minimum Signals

Before scaling, require at least one:

- AddToCart from paid traffic.
- Checkout start from paid traffic.
- Multiple comments asking for price/link.
- Creator post driving clear Shopify sessions.

## Scaling Method

- Increase budget by 20-30%.
- Wait 24-48 hours between increases.
- Duplicate winning creative into a fresh ad set only after stable signal.
- Keep losing creatives paused.

## What Not To Do

- Do not jump from NGN 5,000/day to NGN 30,000/day.
- Do not scale view-only campaigns.
- Do not scale before tracking is verified.
- Do not spend reserve budget without AddToCart signal.
