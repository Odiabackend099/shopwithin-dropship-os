## Required Creator Instructions

- Use `#ad`.
- Keep video 10-20 seconds.
- Hook in first 2 seconds.
- Show pet-hair problem before product.
- Include product page link in bio or caption if possible.
- Send screenshot after posting.
- Send post URL.

## Creator Selection Checklist

- Recent posts have real comments.
- Creator has pet/home/cleaning audience.
- Creator can explain the product naturally.
- Audience includes US/UK/CA/AU or at least English-speaking buyers.
- No obvious bot engagement.
- No stolen-video-only feed.
