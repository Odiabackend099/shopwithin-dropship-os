# Daily Paid Traffic Ops

## Morning

1. Check Shopify orders.
2. Check Flutterwave payment status.
3. Check TikTok/Meta spend.
4. Check pixel event volume.
5. Check comments for trust/shipping objections.

## During Test

Record:

- Spend.
- Impressions.
- Clicks.
- CTR.
- CPC.
- Product page sessions.
- Add-to-carts.
- Checkouts.
- Purchases.
- Refunds.

## End Of Day

1. Pause losers using kill rules.
2. Keep winners unchanged unless they have stable performance.
3. Write one note on what hook or angle won.
4. Create one new creative variation from the winner.

## Do Not Do

- Do not raise budget just because a post has views.
- Do not run three platforms at once on the first small test.
- Do not optimize from vanity metrics alone.
