# Low-Budget Paid Test Plan

## Budget

Total launch budget: NGN 50,000

- TikTok: 70 percent
- Meta/Instagram: 30 percent

Because the ad accounts should use USD billing, enter budget amounts conservatively and manually convert before launch.

## Launch Rule

Do not launch paid ads until organic content produces at least one of:

- Clear comments asking where to buy.
- Best post reaches 500+ views with above-average watch time.
- Product page clicks from organic posts.
- Add-to-cart activity from organic traffic.

## TikTok First Test

- 1 campaign.
- 1 ad group.
- 2 ads max.
- $2-$4/day equivalent.
- Optimize for `AddToCart` if purchase data is zero.
- Kill fast if CTR is below 0.8 percent or CPC is too high.

## Meta First Test

Do not start with broad Meta cold traffic on this budget. Use Meta for:

- Instagram Reels reuse after organic proof.
- Retargeting site visitors and cart viewers after enough traffic exists.

## Kill Rules

- Pause after $3-$5 equivalent spend if CTR is below 0.8 percent.
- Pause after 100 landing page sessions if add-to-cart rate is below 3 percent.
- Pause after 3 checkout starts with no purchases.
- Pause if contribution margin after ad spend is negative for 2 consecutive days.

## Scale Rules

- Raise budget only after CTR is above 1.5 percent and add-to-cart rate is above 3 percent.
- Increase daily budget by 20-30 percent, not by doubling.
- Do not run more than two cold creatives at once during the lean phase.
