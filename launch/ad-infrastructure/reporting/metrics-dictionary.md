# Metrics Dictionary

## Core Metrics

- CAC: customer acquisition cost. `ad spend / purchases`.
- ROAS: return on ad spend. `revenue / ad spend`.
- CTR: click-through rate. `clicks / impressions`.
- CPC: cost per click. `ad spend / clicks`.
- CPM: cost per 1,000 impressions. `ad spend / impressions * 1000`.
- ATC rate: add-to-cart rate. `add_to_carts / product_page_sessions`.
- Checkout rate: `checkout_starts / product_page_sessions`.
- Purchase conversion rate: `purchases / product_page_sessions`.
- AOV: average order value. `revenue / orders`.
- Gross margin: `(revenue - product cost - shipping cost) / revenue`.
- Contribution margin: `revenue - product cost - shipping cost - ad spend - refunds`.

## First Test Benchmarks

- CTR target: above 1.5 percent.
- Kill CTR: below 0.8 percent after meaningful spend.
- ATC target: above 3 percent after 100 sessions.
- CPC target: below $1.00 equivalent if the traffic is cold.
- Purchase target: at least one paid order before scaling.

## Interpretation

- High CTR + low ATC: landing page, product-market fit, or trust issue.
- Low CTR + high watch time: weak CTA or wrong audience.
- Good ATC + no checkout: shipping, price, payment, or trust issue.
- Checkout + no purchase: payment friction, unexpected shipping, or customer hesitation.
