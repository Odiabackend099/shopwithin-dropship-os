# FurLift UGC Video Script - 3 Part Series
**Character:** Sarah (Pet Mom)
**Total Length:** 30-45 seconds (3 parts x 10-15 seconds each)
**Flow AI Credits:** 30 total (10 per part)

## Part 1: The Problem (10-15 seconds)
**Hook:** "I was about to throw away my couch..."

**Script:**
"I was about to throw away my couch. Look at this - it's completely covered in dog hair. I've tried everything - lint rollers, vacuums, even those rubber brushes. Nothing works. My dog Max sheds like crazy and I was honestly embarrassed to have people over. I thought I'd have to buy new furniture or get rid of him."

**Visual Direction:**
- Start: Sarah sitting on a couch covered in visible dog hair, looking frustrated
- Mid: Close-up of the dog hair on the couch fabric
- End: Sarah looking defeated, hand on her forehead

**Character Consistency:**
- Sarah: 30s woman, casual comfortable clothes, messy bun
- Setting: Living room with visible pet hair problem
- Dog: Golden retriever or similar shedding breed visible in background

## Part 2: The Discovery (10-15 seconds)
**Hook:** "Then I found this 3-second hack..."

**Script:**
"Then my sister told me about this thing called FurLift. I was skeptical - another 'miracle' product, right? But she showed me how it works and I was shocked. It's not a vacuum, not a lint roller - it's this special fabric tool that literally lifts the hair off in seconds. Watch this."

**Visual Direction:**
- Start: Sarah holding the FurLift product, looking curious
- Mid: Close-up of the FurLift tool being shown
- End: Sarah smiling, about to demonstrate

**Character Consistency:**
- Same Sarah character, same outfit (or slight variation)
- Same living room setting
- Product: FurLift tool clearly visible
- Dog still visible in background

## Part 3: The Solution (10-15 seconds)
**Hook:** "My couch looks brand new..."

**Script:**
"One swipe and look at that - the hair just lifts right off! No sticky residue, no damaging the fabric. My couch literally looks brand new. I did the whole couch in under 5 minutes. Max still sheds like crazy, but now I don't care. If you're dealing with pet hair, you need this. Link in bio!"

**Visual Direction:**
- Start: Sarah demonstrating FurLift on the couch
- Mid: Close-up of hair being removed from fabric
- End: Clean couch, Sarah smiling with dog, pointing to link

**Character Consistency:**
- Same Sarah character
- Same living room, now clean
- Same dog, now happy
- Product demonstration clearly visible
