## Supplier Acceptance Rules
- Two supplier offers preferred, or one verified fast supplier with inventory.
- Target delivery: `<= 10 business days` for first target market.
- No supplier auto-pay until sample order and tracking sync pass.
- Reject listings with vague material specs, no tracking, fragile parts, or copied branded packaging.
- Do not claim delivery speed publicly until the selected supplier confirms it.

## Page Copy

### Hero
Pet hair should not own your couch.

### Subheadline
Lift embedded dog and cat hair from couches, rugs, car seats, bedding, and pet beds without sticky sheets or batteries.

### CTA
Clean the couch

### Problem Section
If you live with pets, you already know the cycle: clean the couch, turn around, and the fur is back. Sticky rollers run out. Vacuums miss the hair buried in fabric. FurLift gives pet owners a quick manual tool for the daily mess pets leave behind.

### Benefits
- Pulls visible fur from fabric in seconds.
- Reusable design with no refill sheets.
- Manual tool with no charging or batteries.
- Small enough for the car, couch, laundry room, and travel bag.
- Made for repeat cleanups after shedding, grooming, or daily pet naps.

### FAQ
**What surfaces does it work on?**  
Fabric couches, rugs, car seats, bedding, pet beds, and clothing. Test gently on delicate fabrics first.

**Does it need refills?**  
No. It is reusable and does not use sticky sheets.

**Does it work for cats and dogs?**  
Yes. The launch positioning targets households with cat or dog hair on fabric surfaces.

**How long is shipping?**  
Use tracked delivery. Publish the exact ETA only after the supplier is verified.

**Can I return it?**  
Returns follow the store policy and must be handled manually during the validation phase.

## Paid Test Gate
Do not turn on `PAID_ADS_ENABLED` until:
- `>= 15` organic posts are live.
- `>= 20` creatives are produced.
- Best organic CTR is `>= 1.5%` or one video passes `1,000` views with site clicks.
- Product page sessions and add-to-cart behavior are visible.

If the gate passes, run TikTok at `NGN 3,000-NGN 5,000/day` max. Retargeting stays reserved until there is enough audience data.
