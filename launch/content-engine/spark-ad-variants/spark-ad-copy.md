# Spark Ad Copy

## Variant A: Problem First

- Hook: Pet hair on everything?
- Primary text: Pet hair on couches, clothes, and car seats gets old fast. FurLift is a reusable detailer built for the spots sticky rollers struggle with.
- CTA: Shop now
- Best use: Cold TikTok Spark Ad after organic post gets saves, comments, or above-average watch time.

## Variant B: Transformation First

- Hook: Watch the fur line.
- Primary text: The before/after is the whole pitch. Quick pet hair cleanup for couches, fabric seats, blankets, and daily mess.
- CTA: See it in action
- Best use: Retargeting viewers who watched at least 50 percent of a previous demo.

## Variant C: POV Style

- Hook: POV: your dog owns the couch.
- Primary text: If your pet sheds everywhere, this is the kind of small cleanup tool you keep close.
- CTA: Check it out
- Best use: Broad pet-owner interest audiences.

## Variant D: Reaction Style

- Hook: I gasped at this.
- Primary text: I did not expect that much fur to come off the couch. Reusable, simple, and satisfying.
- CTA: Try FurLift
- Best use: Organic-first post, then boost if comments mention the demo.

## Variant E: Pet-Owner Emotion

- Hook: Embarrassing couch fur?
- Primary text: Quick couch reset before guests see the pet hair. No fake miracle, just a reusable tool for visible fur cleanup.
- CTA: Shop FurLift
- Best use: Retargeting cart viewers and site visitors.
