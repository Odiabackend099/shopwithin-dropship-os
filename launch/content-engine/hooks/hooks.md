# FurLift Hook Library

## Top 10 Hooks

1. Pet hair on everything?
2. Watch what came off my couch.
3. Lint rollers miss this.
4. POV: your dog owns the couch.
5. I thought this couch was clean.
6. This is why your sofa still looks dusty.
7. If you have a shedding pet, watch this.
8. The most satisfying couch clean I have tried.
9. Guest coming over and the couch looks like this?
10. This pulled up fur I could not even see.

## Use Order

1. Start with `Pet hair on everything?` for broad cold traffic.
2. Use `Watch what came off my couch` when the thumbnail clearly shows heavy fur.
3. Use `Lint rollers miss this` for comparison-style posts.
4. Use `POV: your dog owns the couch` for pet-owner relatability.
5. Use `Guest coming over...` for urgency without fake scarcity.

## Hook Rules

- Keep the first on-screen text under 6 words when possible.
- Show fur before the product name.
- Do not open with the brand name.
- Do not use fake review language.
- Do not overclaim that it works on every fabric.
