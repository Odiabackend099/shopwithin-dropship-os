## Operating Files

- `metadata.json`: machine-readable campaign metadata.
- `posting-schedule.csv`: first five days of exact posting order.
- `30-day-content-plan.csv`: month-long content testing loop.
- `launch-plan.md`: NGN 50,000 organic-to-Spark launch plan.
- `hooks/hooks.md`: hook library.
- `captions/`: captions, CTAs, and text overlays.
- `thumbnails/`: generated frames and thumbnail guidance.
- `analytics-notes/`: source analysis, scoring, and visual QA.

## Execution Rule

Post organically first. Only boost the best TikTok organic post after it proves watch time, comments, saves, or clicks.

## Watermark Handling

The source video had a baked-in TikTok watermark. Final exports cover that lower-right area with a FurLift sticker so the videos are cleaner for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and Spark Ad reuse.
