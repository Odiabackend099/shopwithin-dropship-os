## Winning Signals

- 3-second hold above 35 percent.
- Average watch time above 5.5 seconds on a 9.7-second video.
- CTR above 1.5 percent on boosted TikTok tests.
- CPC below $1.00 equivalent.
- Add-to-cart rate above 3 percent after 100 product page sessions.

## Losing Signals

- First-frame swipe behavior: low 2-3 second hold.
- Comments say the product is confusing or looks like a normal lint roller.
- Clicks happen but no add-to-carts after 100 sessions.
- CPC rises while watch time stays weak.
