# CTA Variations

1. Try FurLift today.
2. Get the reusable pet hair detailer.
3. Clean the couch before guests notice.
4. Stop buying sticky refills.

## Usage Notes

- Use soft CTAs for organic posts.
- Use direct CTAs only after the demo has shown the fur removal.
- Avoid fake urgency, fake scarcity, or fake review wording.
- Keep price mentions simple: `FurLift - $24.95`.
