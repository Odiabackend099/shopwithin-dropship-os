# FurLift Low-Budget Launch Plan

## Budget

Total: NGN 50,000

- TikTok: 70 percent, NGN 35,000
- Instagram Reels: 30 percent, NGN 15,000
- Paid ads remain off until organic signal appears.

## Organic-First Posting Order

1. TikTok: `furlift-tiktok-v1-problem-first.mp4`
2. Instagram Reels: `furlift-reels-v1-transformation-first.mp4`
3. YouTube Shorts: `furlift-shorts-v1-proof-demo.mp4`
4. TikTok: `furlift-spark-c-pov-style.mp4`
5. Instagram Reels: `furlift-spark-e-pet-owner-emotion.mp4`
6. TikTok: `furlift-spark-d-reaction-style.mp4`
7. TikTok: repost the best hook with a new caption if the first 24 hours show saves or comments.

## Best US-Focused Posting Times

Use Lagos time for execution:

- 2:00 PM WAT - reaches US morning/late morning.
- 6:00 PM WAT - reaches US midday and UK evening.
- 10:00 PM WAT - reaches US afternoon/evening.

Do not post all variants at once. Give each post at least 4-6 hours before judging early signals.

## Spark Ads Transition

Only boost after organic proof:

- Minimum 500 organic views, or
- More than 8 percent engagement rate, or
- Comments asking where to buy, or
- Above-average retention compared to the other FurLift posts.

Start with the best organic TikTok post as a Spark Ad. Use NGN 3,000-5,000 per day. Do not run more than two cold variants at the same time on this budget.

## Kill Rules

Kill or pause a paid test if:

- Spend reaches NGN 5,000 and CTR is below 0.8 percent.
- 100 product page sessions produce fewer than 3 add-to-carts.
- 3 checkout starts produce zero purchases.
- CPC is above $1.00 equivalent and watch time is weak.
- Comments indicate trust, shipping, or product confusion.

## Scaling Rules

Scale only when:

- CTR is above 1.5 percent.
- Add-to-cart rate is above 3 percent.
- The same creative gets comments/saves organically.
- Checkout is still working in Flutterwave.
- Supplier auto-pay remains off until a real shipment is physically verified.

Increase winning budget gradually by 20-30 percent, not all at once.

## Sound Recommendations

- Use TikTok Commercial Music Library audio for TikTok organic and paid usage.
- Pick a currently trending `cleaning reset`, `wait for it`, or `satisfying scrape` sound inside the app at upload time.
- Keep original demo sound low under the music so the cleaning action still feels real.
- For Reels and Shorts, use platform-native audio selected on the day of posting.
