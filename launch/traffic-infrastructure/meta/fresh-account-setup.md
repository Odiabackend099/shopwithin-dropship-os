# Meta Fresh Account Setup

## Goal

Create a low-friction Meta/Instagram launch path owned by the operator.

## Required Assets

- Fresh or clean Facebook account controlled by the operator.
- Facebook Page: `Shopwithin`.
- Instagram business/professional account: `shopwithin` or closest available variant.
- Meta business portfolio: `shopwithin`.
- Meta ad account.
- Meta pixel/dataset.
- Shopify Facebook & Instagram sales channel connected.

## Setup Order

1. Create or log into the fresh Facebook account.
2. Create Facebook Page:
   - Name: `Shopwithin`
   - Category: `Shopping & retail`
   - Bio: `Reusable pet hair cleanup tools and practical home products for pet owners.`
3. Create/convert Instagram profile to professional/business.
4. Link Instagram to the Facebook Page.
5. Open Shopify Admin > Sales channels > Facebook & Instagram.
6. Connect Facebook account.
7. Select/create the `shopwithin` business portfolio.
8. Select/create the `Shopwithin` Facebook Page.
9. Select/create the Meta ad account.
10. Create/connect Meta pixel.
11. Set customer data sharing to `Maximum` if the operator accepts the privacy-policy implications.
12. Verify events in Meta Events Manager.

## Tracking Events

Required:

- PageView
- ViewContent
- AddToCart
- InitiateCheckout
- Purchase

## Spend Safety

- Do not add payment until pixel events are verified.
- Do not launch campaigns from Shopify shortcuts.
- Build campaigns in Ads Manager after assets are stable.
- Start with NGN 3,000-5,000/day or equivalent.
