# Account Safety SOP

## Do

- Use true personal or business details.
- Keep one clean device/browser profile per platform.
- Keep billing/payment under operator control.
- Keep daily budgets low until conversion events verify.
- Screenshot account warnings and stop.
- Use platform support for verification blocks.

## Do Not

- Do not fake US identity or address.
- Do not use rapid VPN country switching.
- Do not create multiple rejected TikTok accounts in a row.
- Do not buy hacked/rented ad accounts.
- Do not fund a blocked account.
- Do not launch ads from shortcut flows without checking budgets.

## When Blocked

1. Screenshot the exact platform error.
2. Record account ID and error code.
3. Stop spending.
4. Use official support/review paths.
5. Shift budget to organic/creators while the platform issue is resolved.
