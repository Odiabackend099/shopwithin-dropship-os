# First Paid Traffic SOP

## Before Spending

- Product page opens on mobile.
- Product price shows USD.
- Checkout works.
- Pixel PageView/ViewContent fires.
- Pixel AddToCart fires.
- UTM URL works.
- Daily campaign budget cap is set.
- Payment method added by operator only.

## Launch Sequence

1. Post organic video.
2. Watch first 4-12 hours.
3. Use the best organic post as the paid/boost candidate.
4. Spend NGN 2,000-5,000 max on first paid test.
5. Check Shopify sessions by UTM.
6. Check AddToCart.
7. Kill weak creative quickly.

## Reporting Cadence

Every 12 hours:

- Spend.
- Sessions.
- CTR.
- CPC.
- AddToCart.
- Checkout starts.
- Purchases.
- Comments/trust objections.

## First Success Definition

The first win is not profit.

The first win is:

```text
paid/creator traffic -> product sessions -> add-to-cart
```

After add-to-cart exists, optimize checkout and retargeting.
