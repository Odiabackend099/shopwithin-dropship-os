## Risk Mitigation

**Risk:** Existing pet customers confused by broader product mix
**Mitigation:** 
- Email announcement explaining expansion
- Keep pet products prominently featured
- Create "Pet Favorites" collection
- Offer exclusive discount to existing customers

**Risk:** Too many categories dilutes brand focus
**Mitigation:**
- Start with 10 products across 9 categories
- Scale based on performance data
- Pause underperforming categories quickly
- Focus marketing on top-performing categories

**Risk:** Supplier issues with new product types
**Mitigation:**
- Test each supplier with small orders first
- Have backup suppliers ready
- Use Zendrop/Spocket for supplier flexibility
- Monitor supplier performance closely
