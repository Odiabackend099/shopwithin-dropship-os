# Standard Operating Procedures

## Daily
- Review product discovery queue.
- Approve only products with score `>= 78` and all supplier gates passed.
- Check failed webhooks, dead-letter queue, supplier failures, delayed shipments, refunds, and chargebacks.
- Review ad kill-switch recommendations.
- Publish 3-5 short-form organic videos for the one active test product during lean launch mode.
- Check creative validation status before any paid spend: minimum `15` organic posts, `20` creatives, and real click/view signal.
- Keep TikTok cold testing at or below `NGN 5,000/day`; if the gate blocks spend, create more hooks instead of forcing ads.

## Product Launch
1. Confirm supplier cost, shipping cost, delivery SLA, tracking support, and market coverage.
2. Generate product page and creative assets.
3. Publish as Shopify draft.
4. QA mobile page, SEO metadata, JSON-LD, payment methods, and shipping copy.
5. Activate product only after manual review.
6. Start organic traffic before paid traffic.

## NGN 50,000 Lean Launch
1. Spend `NGN 10,000` on domain and basic tools only.
2. Spend `NGN 10,000` on AI creative production and editing support.
3. Hold `NGN 20,000` for TikTok micro-tests after validation.
4. Hold `NGN 10,000` for retargeting, backups, or operational recovery.
5. Do not spend the full budget in one campaign or one day.
6. Do not start broad Meta cold prospecting before TikTok/Reels creative validation.
7. Stop a product when the kill-switch fires; do not keep paying for a product with no clicks or no add-to-cart signal.

## Creative Validation Gate
1. Produce 20-30 short-form creative variants for the selected product.
2. Publish at least 15 organic posts across TikTok, Reels, Shorts, and Pinterest.
3. Track best-video views, click-through rate, product page sessions, and add-to-carts.
4. Permit paid TikTok testing only when the creative gate passes.
5. Cap approved cold test spend to `NGN 3,000-NGN 5,000/day`.
6. Retarget video viewers, site visitors, and cart abandoners only after there is enough traffic to form an audience.

## Order Handling
1. Confirm payment status.
2. Confirm risk level.
3. Route to selected fast supplier.
4. Sync tracking to Shopify.
5. Send customer updates through Shopify/Klaviyo.
6. Log profit snapshot.

## Customer Support
- Use Shopify Inbox first.
- AI can answer shipping, tracking, product basics, and policy questions.
- Human review is required for refunds, cancellations, address changes, chargebacks, and angry customers.
