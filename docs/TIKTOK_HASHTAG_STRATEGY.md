## Hashtag Best Practices

### DO
- **Relevance First:** Only use hashtags genuinely related to content
- **Consistency:** Use similar hashtag mixes for comparable content
- **Testing:** Regularly test new hashtag combinations
- **Monitoring:** Track performance and adjust based on data
- **Quality over Quantity:** 3-5 relevant hashtags better than 10 irrelevant ones

### DON'T
- **Overuse:** Don't use more than 5-6 hashtags per video
- **Irrelevance:** Avoid hashtags unrelated to your content
- **Banned Hashtags:** Research and avoid flagged/banned hashtags
- **Spam:** Don't use the same hashtags in every single video
- **Trend Chasing:** Don't use trending hashtags that don't fit your content

## Banned/Flagged Hashtags to Avoid

### Commonly Flagged Categories
- **Adult Content:** Any hashtags with adult connotations
- **Violence:** Hashtags related to violence or harm
- **Illegal Activities:** Any hashtags suggesting illegal behavior
- **Spam:** Overused hashtags associated with spam accounts
- **Misleading:** Hashtags that don't match content

### Research Before Using
- Search hashtag on TikTok to see content quality
- Check if hashtag appears in "banned hashtag" lists
- Monitor if your content gets shadowbanned after using certain hashtags
- Start with safe, established hashtags in your niche

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Overusing Hashtags
**Problem:** Using 10+ hashtags per video
**Solution:** Stick to 3-5 relevant, high-quality hashtags

### Mistake 2: Irrelevant Hashtags
**Problem:** Using popular hashtags unrelated to content
**Solution:** Prioritize relevance over popularity

### Mistake 3: Same Hashtags Every Video
**Problem:** Using identical hashtag mix for all content
**Solution:** Tailor hashtags to specific content type and topic

### Mistake 4: Ignoring Performance Data
**Problem:** Not tracking which hashtags perform best
**Solution:** Regularly review and adjust based on performance metrics

### Mistake 5: Chasing Trends Blindly
**Problem:** Using trending hashtags that don't fit content
**Solution:** Only use trends when genuinely relevant to your content
